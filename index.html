Oilfield services

ERR is an oilfield service company strategically located in central Oklahoma. We offer a diverse range of services and take pride in our team of highly skilled and experienced workers. With a cumulative experience of over 50 years in the oilfield industry, we are committed to providing top-quality services to our clients.

Exacting standards

ERR aspires to thrive alongside Oklahoma's expanding oilfield industry. We hold ourselves to exacting standards, ensuring that our personnel consistently deliver top-quality work that exceeds our customers' expectations.

Emphasis on safety

Our mission is to prioritize safety while delivering the highest quality service at affordable rates.

What we can do for you

  • full list of capabilities

  • Dirt work

    New and existing pipe exposure, location pad construction, and maintenance of lease roads.

  • Steel & Poly Welding

    Certified and experienced welders for all poly and steel lines, new pig trap fabrication, and leak repair completion.

  • Line locating

    We offer line locating services with experienced locators for electrical, poly, and steel pipelines, as well as hydrovac and line exposure.

  • Concrete work

    Including finishing, pad construction for vessels, pumps, cattle guards, and driveway and access road completion.

  • Construction

    Location tear down and rebuild, equipment hauling, new location pad and tank battery construction, pumping unit tear down and rebuild, and compressor hook up and tear down.

  • Contract operator

    With over 50 years of experience, we provide contract operator services for all oilfield facilities, including production, pipeline, midstream, cryogenic, and plant facilities. Our services also include maintenance and pigging of all types of pipelines, such as crude, gas, water, steel, and poly, as well as pipe fitting, power washing, and steaming.

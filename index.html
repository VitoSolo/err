Construction Services

East River Roustabout, centrally located in Oklahoma, provides construction services specifically tailored for oilfield projects. Our team consists of highly skilled and experienced professionals who offer a wide range of services in construction, maintenance, and operations, focusing on both upstream and midstream sectors.

Exacting standards

East River provides industry leading roustabout services to Oklahoma's ever expanding energy industry. We understand reputation is built over years, and can be lost with a single experience. East River expects to exceed our customers' expectations with every job...on time...the first time.

Emphasis on safety

East River emerged as a response to the frustrations experienced by our affiliate upstream operator. They observed that roustabout services were frequently treated as an afterthought, provided by unsafe, under-skilled, and under-capitalized service providers. These providers seemed more focused on maximizing profits for individual jobs rather than consistently delivering high-quality, safe performance for the essential small-scale tasks that arise day after day.
